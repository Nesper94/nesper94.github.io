Translations of papers to Spanish

Science students often struggle to learn a lot of concepts and theory developed by centuries in just a few years, and for non-native english speakers, learn those concepts while trying to master a new language can be an important obstacle. For this reason I have initiated a personal project to translate seminal papers in the history of biology in order to make science more accesible to Spanish speakers.
